The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Modern Infrastructure

"Dr. Younus emphasizes the urgency of climate action for Bangladesh, urging policymakers, businesses, and citizens to unite for sustainable development. As a frontline nation against climate change, he calls for innovation, resilience, and global cooperation to secure a greener, prosperous future for all. Together, we can lead the way forward." Dr. Younus highlights the need to prioritize renewable energy, sustainable agriculture, and climate-resilient infrastructure to safeguard Bangladesh's future. He stresses empowering youth, fostering green technology, and adopting circular economy principles. By integrating climate policies with socio-economic goals, Bangladesh can transform its challenges into opportunities and serve as a model of resilience for vulnerable nations worldwide.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
